Overview

V is a RSS reader built with React Native. Completely free to use, open source and my personal daily go-to app to read news from. Currently available to download from Google Play and Github.

Features

  • Ability to display max. 150 news from different sources at once on homescreen.
  • Dark mode.
  • Multiple themes.
  • Categorize RSS feeds.
  • Rename, enable/disable and delete RSS feeds.
  • Rename and delete categories.
  • Ads free.
  • Privacy safe - everything is saved locally on device.
  • Stats - current and best streak, first use of app and number of news opened.

How it works

Whole concept of V is based around RSS feeds. User can add new RSS feeds by submitting proper RSS link - then name, id and link of that feed is saved onto device (using AsyncStorage). Everytime when user visits homescreen, in background array of feeds is loaded from AsyncStorage and then every feed (that's enabled) is fetched - this gives us responses which contains informations about every news that's saved on that RSS feed (title, date published, etc.) - by using this informations we can create proper cards for news that comes from that feed.
